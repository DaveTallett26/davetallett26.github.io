Cookies


On 26th May 2012 the EU Directive on cookies came into force in the UK.

The two pages Cookies and Disabling Cookies are taken from FT.com and provide a good description of how cookies are used on a major website and how to disable them should you so wish.

Industrial Computer Contracts (West Midlands) Ltd uses cookies on this website, for example by the use of Google Analytics. They help us to know a little bit about you and how you use our website, which improves your browsing experience and our marketing - both for you and for others. They are stored locally on your computer or mobile device. To accept cookies continue browsing as normal, otherwise go to the Disabling Cookies page for more information on how to amend your preferences.

This page is from the UK Government website and describes how they use Google Analytics to track who uses their website.

I found Avinash Kaushik's blog useful in understanding the background to cookies and their uses.

Ghostery will provide you with a browser add-on which tracks the use of cookies on your computer in real time.

My Great Grandfather
Provided by Colin, via ancestry.co.uk, May 2009.

Richard Tallett born in 1841 in Newnham, Northamptonshire.

His brother Joseph born in 1839 married Hannah Chamberlain in 1861. They had one child Joseph. Joseph (Snr) died in 1862.

In the 1871 census Richard is living with Hannah and they marry in 1872. There are 4 children born between 1862-1871, before the marriage and 5 more children born after the marriage. The family moves to Leicestershire and they are together through 1871-1901 censuses.

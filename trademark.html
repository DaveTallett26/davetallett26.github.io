Dave Tallett - Trademark


In the top left hand corner of this page is the graphic to be used as a trade mark. It is intended as the stylised representation of the name Tallett. Thus the letters are represented as the binary digits 1011011 and the bar over the top of the graphic represents the strikethrough as would be used in a handwritten signature. The main business of Dave Tallett is in computer consultancy services which is why the binary digits are used. It is intended to be used on a web page similar to this one or on invoices and business cards.

My Grandmother
Found on FamilySearch.org June 2022.

Kate Whinney was born in 1885 in the United Kingdom.

She married Frederick Tallett on the 16th June 1909. (England and Wales Marriage Registration Index, 1837-2005)

The family lived at Braybrooke Road, Little Bowden, Market Harborough, Leicestershire.
The house is now called The Old Malt House

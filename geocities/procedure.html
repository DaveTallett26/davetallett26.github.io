Levels of Organisation

Level 1 - The organisation has a software development procedure

The simplest way to look at this would be that the organisation has standards and practices for software development, for example a naming convention. They may be written down or passed by word of mouth by management.

Level 2 - The procedure is ingrained

If the procedure is ingrained the software developers in the organisation will develop software using the standard customs and practices without thinking. They will explain it to new starters without referring to manuals most of the time.

The procedure is measurable

There is a feedback loop to continuously improve the process

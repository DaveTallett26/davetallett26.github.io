The Tallett
An old apple store, The Tallett is a barn conversion, in the peaceful hamlet of Marwood, overlooking meadowland and the 12th century Marwood Church spire. Veranda and stable door opening into open plan style accommodation, lounge/dining area with colour TV. Well equipped kitchenette, with gas hob, microwave/combination oven and fridge. One bedroom with double bed and duvet. Shower room with toilet and washbasin. Single bed available. Very tastefully furnished and decorated by caring owners. In an idyllic setting overlooking a fresh water pond, where guests can enjoy splended scenic walks or take a picnic in the grounds.

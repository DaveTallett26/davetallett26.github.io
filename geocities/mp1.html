Talk to your MP

Use your postcode to find out who your MP is with the House of Commons Constituency Locata.

The BBC People in Parliament guide will give you a quick introduction to your MP.

Use my MPs on the Web page to find an MP with a personal web site.

The Lib Dem MPs have individual pages on a common host but they all have individual email addresses.

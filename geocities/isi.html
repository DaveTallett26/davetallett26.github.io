Information Society Initiative

The Department of Trade and Industry is sponsoring the Information Society Initiative designed to acheive the aim of making the UK the hub of e-commerce in Europe. Their web site provides some email addresses so that we can alert them to the effect of IR35. They have said that they are representing our point of view to the Treasury and the Inland Revenue so they are on our side.

Doc

Doc is my nickname, Doc is one of Snow White's seven dwarfs, hence the Doc graphic in the top left hand corner.

How did I get the nickname, apart from the fact that I have a PhD and therefore I AM Dr Tallett ?

It started when I worked at Pottertons where the Business Systems Manager Alan Alcock had a habit of saying he was going to consult Dr Tallett.

The footballers at Pottertons, particularly Barry Masters (Baz, the man with the biggest collection of flick knives in Blaken) then adopted the name Doc.

To cap it all I worked with Linda Robinson's Support Group which consisted of seven men and Linda so it was ideal for a Christmas competition where the rest of the department had to guess who was which dwarf if Linda was Snow White. I was Doc of course.

I'm a doctor, you can trust me!

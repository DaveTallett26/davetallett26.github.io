About

I am a retired developer formerly working with Microsoft Access, Excel, VBA and SQL Server. Over a career spanning more than 40 years I have worked in most roles — developer, DBA, and information analyst.

My clients have spanned the Public Sector, engineering and media, primarily in the Midlands, with notable engagements at National Grid, Jaguar Land Rover, Severn Trent, Warwickshire County Council, and many others.

I haven't worked on a full-time contract since 2016, and with the introduction of IR35 across the private sector in 2021, I now look to work on PAYE Fixed Term Contracts only. My limited company, Industrial Computer Contracts (West Midlands) Ltd, operated for 39 years before closing in 2022.

To me the best software is easily understood and simple — even if it may be long-winded. Straightforward, reliable solutions that do exactly what they need to do.

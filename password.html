Make my password more secure

Here are some tips on how to make your password more secure

Avoid your name or birthday.
Don't use sequences or repeated characters. For example, 12345678 or qwerty.
Combine letters, numbers, and symbols.
Use combinations of characters that are not in the dictionary.
Make it long. Passwords normally can be 6-12 characters long, the longer it is, the more secure it will be.
Use words and phrases that are difficult for people to guess.
Use punctuation - for example put a full stop at the end.
Use the first letters of a phrase or saying that you will remember for example
Beverly Crusher: Click my heels together three times and I'm back in Kansas - can it be that simple?- CmhtttaIbiKcibts?
Use a random password generator - random.org/passwords
